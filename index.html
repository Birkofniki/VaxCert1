2. VaxCert

Hint-Digital vaccination certificates stored on a blockchain to verify immunization status across borders.

What it is?

I am proposing a new blockchain project called VaxCert that would create digital vaccination certificates stored on a blockchain ledger. This would allow individuals to easily verify their immunization status when crossing borders or accessing services that require proof of vaccination.

Its advantages?

  • Increased trust and integrity of vaccination records
  • Reduced risk of fraudulent or forged paper certificates
  • Seamless verification process for vaccination requirements
  • User convenience - records accessible anytime from an app
  • Helps reopen travel and economy by enabling proof of vaccination
  • Potential to integrate with other health records for comprehensive view.
